Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LullwaveFM ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our automated music publishing service.

1. Information We Collect

This application does not collect personal information from TikTok users. The only data accessed through TikTok's API is:

  • Access tokens required to authenticate and publish content to the LullwaveFM TikTok account
  • Basic account information (username, account ID) needed to verify the publishing target

2. How We Use Information

The information accessed is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate the LullwaveFM account with TikTok's Content Posting API
  • Publish AI-generated ambient music videos to the LullwaveFM TikTok channel

We do not use this information for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

OAuth access tokens are stored locally on our private server and are never shared with third parties. Tokens are used solely to authenticate API requests to TikTok on behalf of the LullwaveFM account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. All API interactions are between our private server and TikTok's official API endpoints.

5. Third-Party Services

This service integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API. TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs how TikTok handles data on their platform. Please refer to TikTok's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect access tokens and account credentials. Access is restricted to authorized systems only.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the LullwaveFM TikTok account.
