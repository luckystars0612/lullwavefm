Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: March 2026

Lullwave FM ("we", "our", or "us") operates a multi-platform AI ambient music service, including YouTube channels, TikTok (@lullwave.official), Facebook Page (LullwaveFM), Instagram (@lullwavefm), and this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our services.

1. Information We Collect

Lullwave FM does not directly collect personal data from website visitors. This is a static informational website. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics that identify individual users.

When you interact with our content on third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Spotify, etc.), those platforms' own privacy policies apply to any data they collect.

2. TikTok Integration

We use the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish short ambient music videos to our official TikTok account (@lullwave.official). Through this integration:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any TikTok user data.
  • We do not access follower information, DMs, or any personal data.
  • The API is used exclusively for automated video publishing by our internal system.
  • No third parties receive any data from our TikTok API usage.

3. Facebook Integration

We use the Facebook Graph API solely to publish short ambient music videos as Reels to our official Facebook Page (LullwaveFM). Through this integration:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any Facebook user data.
  • We do not access follower information, messages, or any personal data.
  • The API is used exclusively for automated video publishing by our internal system.
  • We only request permissions necessary for video publishing: pages_manage_posts, pages_read_engagement, pages_show_list.

4. Instagram Integration

We use the Instagram Graph API solely to publish short ambient music videos as Reels to our official Instagram account (@lullwavefm). Through this integration:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any Instagram user data.
  • We do not access follower information, messages, or any personal data.
  • The API is used exclusively for automated video publishing by our internal system.
  • We only request permissions necessary for video publishing: instagram_basic, instagram_content_publish.

5. Third-Party Platforms

Our music is distributed across YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Spotify, Apple Music, and other streaming services. Each of these platforms operates under its own privacy policy and data practices. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect our systems. Since we do not collect user data, there is no personal data at risk from our website or publishing tools.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
